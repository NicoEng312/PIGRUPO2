Breaking Bad

Breaking Bad is a gripping crime drama that follows the transformation of a high school chemistry teacher after he receives life-changing news. Set in the American Southwest, the series explores themes of morality, survival, and personal evolution, offering a deep and intense look at the choices people make when pushed to their limits. Known for its storytelling, complex characters, and cinematic quality, it's considered one of the greatest TV series of all time.

Year of Release: 2008

Duration: 5 seasons | 62 episodes

Cast: Bryan Cranston, Aaron Paul, Anna Gunn, more

Created by: Vince Gilligan

Genres: Thriller, Crime, Drama, Dark comedy, Classic, more
